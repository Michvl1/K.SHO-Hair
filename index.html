About Me

Crafting confidence, one braid at a time

I'm a passionate independent hair stylist based in Medway, Kent, specialising in protective styles that celebrate the beauty and versatility of natural hair.

Every client who sits in my chair deserves to feel heard, comfortable, and absolutely stunning. I take the time to understand your hair's unique needs and your personal vision before I begin.

Secure deposit

A £15 booking fee is required to secure your appointment. Booking fees are non-refundable and non-transferable unless your appointment is cancelled by KSHO Hair.

Booking Management & Cancellation Policy

Appointments cancelled within 24 hours may be subject to cancellation charges.

Mobile appointments require a minimum of 72 hours' notice.

Home appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled up to 48 hours before the scheduled time.

Emergencies will be considered on a case-by-case basis.
