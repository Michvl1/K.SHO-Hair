About Me

Hello, I'm Keziah — your hair's new best friend.

With over seven years of experience in the hair industry, I am dedicated to creating beautiful, long-lasting styles that help every client feel confident and empowered.

After building a strong foundation within salon environments, I chose to work independently to provide a more personalised and client-focused experience.

Specialising in cornrows, freestyle braids, boho goddess braids and curly sew-ins, my goal is to create a relaxed and welcoming environment where clients can enjoy a professional service from start to finish. I believe getting your hair done should feel effortless — simply arrive, relax and leave feeling your absolute best.
